Frequently Asked Questions

When can I visit the school?

Campus tours are available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We recommend calling ahead to schedule your visit to ensure a staff member is available to show you around and answer all your questions.

How do I apply for admission?

You can obtain an admission form from our school office or download it from our website (when available). Complete the form, attach all required documents, and submit it to our admissions office. Your child will then be scheduled for an entrance assessment.

Do you offer transportation services?

Yes, we offer school bus services to various locations around Port Harcourt. Transportation fees vary depending on the distance from the school. Please contact our admissions office for specific routes and pricing.

What is your student-teacher ratio?

We maintain a low student-teacher ratio to ensure personalized attention. Our average class size is 20-25 students with one main teacher and teaching assistants in nursery and lower primary classes.

Do you provide meals at school?

Yes, we provide nutritious breakfast and lunch for all students. Our meals are prepared fresh daily by our catering team. Special dietary requirements can be accommodated with prior notice to the school administration.

What extracurricular activities do you offer?

We offer a wide range of activities including sports (football, basketball, volleyball, athletics), music, drama, debate, chess club, environmental club, art, and computer coding club. Students can participate based on their interests.
