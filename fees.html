Important Information About School Fees

  • Payment Plans: We offer flexible payment options. Tuition can be paid termly or in full for the academic year with a 5% discount.
  • Sibling Discount: Families with two or more children enrolled receive a 10% discount on tuition fees for the second child and 15% for the third and subsequent children.
  • Early Payment Discount: Parents who pay for the entire academic year before resumption receive a 5% discount on total tuition fees.
  • Payment Methods: We accept bank transfers, cheques, and card payments. Cash payments are accepted only at the school bursar's office.
  • Refund Policy: Admission fees are non-refundable. Tuition fees are refundable only if withdrawal notice is given at least one month before the start of the term.
  • Late Payment: A late payment fee of ₦5,000 applies to fees paid after the second week of the term. Students may not be allowed to sit for examinations if fees are outstanding.
  • What's Included: Tuition covers instruction, use of school facilities, library access, basic school supplies, and participation in regular school activities. Special trips, competitions, and optional activities may incur additional fees.
  • Development Levy: This annual fee covers infrastructure maintenance, technology upgrades, and facility improvements. It is paid only once per academic year with first term fees.
  • Banking Details: Tender Dreams Academy, Account Number: 0123456789, Guaranty Trust Bank (GTB), Iwofe Branch. Please use student's name as payment reference.
