Admissions

Begin your child's journey to excellence at Tender Dreams Academy

Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to be straightforward and transparent. We welcome students who demonstrate academic potential and a willingness to learn.

1

Submit Application

Complete and submit the admission application form online or at our school office. Ensure all required information is accurately provided and all supporting documents are attached.

2

Entrance Assessment

Prospective students will be scheduled for an entrance assessment appropriate to their level. This helps us understand the child's current academic standing and learning needs.

3

Parent Interview

Parents/guardians will meet with school administrators to discuss the school's philosophy, expectations, and answer any questions. This ensures alignment between home and school.

4

Admission Decision

Within one week of the interview, you will receive notification of the admission decision. Successful applicants will be provided with admission letters and next steps.

5

Payment & Registration

Accept the offer by paying the acceptance fee and completing the registration process. New students will receive orientation materials and school calendar.

6

Welcome Orientation

Attend the new student orientation program where your child will meet teachers, tour the facilities, and begin their exciting journey at Tender Dreams Academy.

Admission Requirements

To ensure a smooth admission process, please prepare the following documents:

  • Completed admission application form
  • Student's birth certificate or age declaration
  • Three recent passport photographs of the child
  • Two recent passport photographs of each parent/guardian
  • Last report card or academic records (for transfer students)
  • Transfer certificate from previous school (where applicable)
  • Immunization records and medical report
  • Copy of parent/guardian's valid identification
  • Proof of residence (utility bill or tenancy agreement)

Age Requirements

Playgroup: 2 years and above

Pre-Nursery: 3 years and above

Nursery 1: 4 years and above

Nursery 2: 5 years and above

Primary 1: 6 years and above

Age is calculated as at September 1st of the admission year. Birth certificates or statutory age declarations must be provided for age verification.

Admission Sessions

We accept admissions throughout the year, with major intakes in:

First Term: September - December

Second Term: January - March

Third Term: April - July

Special Programs & Support

Transfer Students

We welcome students transferring from other schools. Transfer students must provide their last report card and a transfer certificate from their previous school. An assessment may be conducted to determine appropriate class placement.

Learning Support

We provide additional support for students who may need extra help in certain subjects. Our dedicated teachers offer remedial classes and personalized attention to ensure every student achieves their potential.

Gifted Students

Students who demonstrate exceptional academic ability are provided with enrichment programs and advanced learning opportunities to keep them challenged and engaged throughout their educational journey.

Important Admission Information

School Calendar

Our academic year runs from September to July, divided into three terms:

First Term: September to December (13 weeks)

Second Term: January to March (11 weeks)

Third Term: April to July (12 weeks)

What to Expect After Admission

Once your child is admitted, you will receive:

  • Admission letter and student handbook
  • Academic calendar and important dates
  • List of required textbooks and materials
  • School uniform requirements and suppliers
  • Information about extracurricular activities
  • Access to our parent portal for online communication
  • Details about orientation program and first day of school

Visiting Our Campus

We encourage prospective parents to visit our school and see our facilities. Campus tours are available Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM. Please call ahead to schedule your visit and ensure a staff member is available to show you around.
