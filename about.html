About Tender Dreams Academy

Excellence in education, innovation in learning, and commitment to developing future leaders

Who We Are

Tender Dreams Academy is a leading private educational institution located in the heart of Iwofe, Port Harcourt, Rivers State. Established with a vision to transform education in Nigeria, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in quality education across the Niger Delta region.

Our school caters to students from nursery through secondary level, providing a comprehensive educational experience that balances academic rigor with character development, technological proficiency, and extracurricular excellence. We pride ourselves on our modern facilities, qualified teaching staff, and a curriculum that prepares students not just for examinations, but for life.

At Tender Dreams Academy, we believe that every child has unique potential waiting to be discovered and nurtured. Our holistic approach to education ensures that students develop intellectually, emotionally, socially, and physically, becoming well-rounded individuals ready to make positive contributions to society.

Our Legacy

Since our establishment, Tender Dreams Academy has consistently produced outstanding results in both internal and external examinations. Our graduates have gained admission to prestigious universities both in Nigeria and abroad, including institutions like the University of Port Harcourt, University of Lagos, Covenant University, and international schools in the United Kingdom, United States, and Canada.

We have received numerous accolades and recognition from educational bodies for our innovative teaching methods, excellent student performance, and contribution to educational development in Rivers State.

Our Core Values

These values guide everything we do and form the foundation of our educational philosophy

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all aspects of education, encouraging students to pursue excellence in academics, character, and all their endeavors.

Innovation

We embrace modern teaching methodologies and technology, preparing students for a rapidly evolving digital world with cutting-edge learning tools and approaches.

Integrity

We instill strong moral values and ethical principles, teaching students the importance of honesty, transparency, and doing what is right even when no one is watching.

Leadership

We develop leadership skills and confidence in our students, empowering them to take initiative, solve problems, and make positive impacts in their communities.

Community

We foster a strong sense of community and collaboration, teaching students the value of teamwork, respect for diversity, and social responsibility.

Discipline

We maintain high standards of discipline and self-control, helping students develop the focus, perseverance, and work ethic needed for success in life.

Our Mission

To provide world-class education that combines academic excellence with moral values, technological proficiency, and leadership skills, empowering students to become innovative thinkers and responsible citizens who positively impact their communities and the world. We are committed to creating a nurturing learning environment where every student is valued, challenged, and supported to reach their full potential.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Rivers State and Nigeria, recognized for producing well-rounded graduates who excel academically, embrace technology, demonstrate strong character, and are equipped to thrive in an ever-changing global landscape. We envision a future where our alumni become leaders, innovators, and changemakers across various fields, making significant contributions to national development and global progress.

Why Choose Tender Dreams Academy?

Academic Excellence

Our curriculum is designed to meet and exceed Nigerian and international educational standards. We employ highly qualified teachers who are passionate about their subjects and committed to student success. Our track record speaks for itself with consistent outstanding performance in WAEC, NECO, and entrance examinations.

Modern Infrastructure

Our campus features state-of-the-art facilities including well-equipped science laboratories, modern computer labs with high-speed internet, a well-stocked library, spacious classrooms with smart boards, and excellent sports facilities for physical development.

Technology Integration

We prepare students for the digital age through comprehensive computer education, coding classes, and the integration of educational technology in teaching. Students gain hands-on experience with modern tools and software, giving them a competitive advantage in higher education and future careers.

Holistic Development

Beyond academics, we offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports, music, drama, debate, quiz competitions, and leadership programs. We believe in developing the whole child - mind, body, and character.

Safe Environment

The safety and wellbeing of our students is our top priority. We maintain a secure campus with trained security personnel, comprehensive safety protocols, and a caring pastoral system that ensures every child feels safe, valued, and supported.

Parental Partnership

We believe education is most effective when there is strong partnership between school and home. We maintain open communication with parents through regular updates, parent-teacher meetings, and involvement in school activities, ensuring collaborative support for each child's development.
